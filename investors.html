SnowApp — Investment Overview

The Problem

Money apps are fragmented and expensive. Wallets, loyalty points, and game balances don’t interoperate. Fees stack up and value gets trapped.

The Solution

SnowApp provides a unified wallet: users load USD, transfer peer-to-peer, spend via virtual/physical card, and withdraw to their bank — all with transparent pricing.

How It Works

  • Users add money by card or bank link.
  • Card spend settles in USD at authorization.
  • Peer-to-peer runs on the internal ledger for speed and lower cost.
  • Partner balances can convert back to USD on withdrawal.

User Plans

  • Tier 1 (Free): Basic account and starter affiliate cap.
  • Tier 2: Paid plan enabling higher limits and caps.
  • Tier 3: Top tier with highest caps and faster payouts.

Market & Ask

U.S. P2P ≈ $1.4T (2025); Global mobile gaming ≈ $111B (2024); Neobank revenue ≈ $5.2B (2025). We’re raising $500k pre-seed to launch MVP and onboard 10k users.
