SnowApp — Investment Overview

The Problem

Money apps are fragmented and expensive. Wallets, loyalty points, and balances don’t interoperate; fees stack up and value gets trapped.

The Solution

SnowApp provides a unified wallet experience: users load USD, transfer peer-to-peer, spend by virtual/physical card, and withdraw to bank—transparent pricing.

How It Works

  • Add money by card or secure bank link.
  • Card spend settles in USD at authorization.
  • P2P activity runs on an internal ledger for speed/cost.
  • Partner balances can convert back to USD on withdrawal.

User Plans

  • Tier 1 (Free): Basic account + starter affiliate cap.
  • Tier 2: Paid plan with higher limits/caps.
  • Tier 3: Highest limits and faster payouts.
