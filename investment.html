SnowApp — Investment Overview

The Problem

Money apps are fragmented and expensive. Wallets, loyalty points, and game balances don’t interoperate. Fees stack up and value gets trapped.

The Solution

SnowApp unifies the wallet experience: load USD, P2P transfers, card spend, and bank withdrawals — with transparent pricing.

How It Works

Funds by card/bank link. Card spend settles in USD at auth. P2P on internal ledger. Partner balances convert to USD on withdrawal.

User Plans

  • Tier 1 (Free): Basic account and starter affiliate cap.
  • Tier 2: Paid plan with higher limits and caps.
  • Tier 3: Highest limits and faster payouts.

Market & Ask

U.S. P2P ≈ $1.4T (2025); Mobile gaming ≈ $111B (2024); Neobank revenue ≈ $5.2B (2025). Raising $500k pre-seed for MVP & first 10k users.
